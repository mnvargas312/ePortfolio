About Me

I am a Learning Design & Technology graduate student with a background in computer science and education. I’m passionate about building meaningful, accessible learning experiences that combine pedagogy, data, and technology.

My interests include instructional design, educational technology, math education, and using data to support students, especially multilingual learners. I’m working toward a career where I can design, build, and support digital learning tools that empower both teachers and students.

Interests

Learning design, educational technology, math pedagogy, UX for learning platforms, and data-informed decision making in schools.

Experience

Experience teaching and tutoring math, building web applications, and collaborating with educators to design technology-integrated lessons.

Goals

Grow as an instructional designer, lead technology initiatives in K–12 or higher education, and create tools that make learning more equitable and engaging.
