Featured Artifact

Car Buying Negotiation Strategies: A Learning-Centered Podcast

This short podcast introduces practical negotiation strategies for purchasing a car at a dealership, with a focus on how to haggle for a fair price. In the episode, I walk listeners through key ideas such as setting a target price, using initial offers strategically, and recognizing when to step away from a deal. I scripted and recorded the episode to be concise, approachable, and clearly organized so that a general audience can follow the concepts even if they are unfamiliar with negotiation or financial terminology.

Creating this podcast helped me apply foundational instructional design knowledge to the collaborative use of content, pedagogy, and technology. I had to decide what prior knowledge to assume, how to chunk the information into a logical sequence, and how to use tone, pacing, and emphasis to support listener understanding rather than overwhelm them. The project strengthened my ability to translate complex, real-world processes into clear instructional messages, while also considering how an audio format can support engagement and accessibility for diverse learners.
